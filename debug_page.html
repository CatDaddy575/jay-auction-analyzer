Roush-Supercharged 6k-Mile 2021 Ford F-150 SVT Lightning Tribute

This 2021 Ford F-150 XL is the third SVT Lightning Tribute example produced by Pioneer Ford of Bremen, Georgia, and it has been driven 6k miles. The build includes a 650 horsepower-rated Roush Phase 2 supercharger system, an aftermarket air-intake system, an aluminum intercooler, a 60mm dual-bore throttle body, and Roush high-flow fuel injectors for the 5.0-liter Coyote V8. Other SVT Lightning-inspired details included with the package are a 3″ MBRP cat-back exhaust system with side-exit outlets, a lowered suspension, 22″ five-spoke wheels, and light gray sueded microfiber and black leather upholstery. Further equipment includes a 10-speed automatic transmission, an electronically locking rear differential, the Trailer Tow Package, the XL Power Equipment Group, Race Red paint, a folding tonneau cover, a carpeted bedliner, and Ford Co-Pilot 360 2.0. The truck resided with its original owner until it was purchased in 2026 by the seller. This F-150 SVT Lightning Tribute is now offered with a digital window sticker, a damage-free Carfax report, and a clean Connecticut title in the seller’s name.

The SVT Lightning Tribute was created in 2017 by the General Manager of Pioneer Ford of Bremen, Georgia, Robby Winiarczyk, to commemorate the 1993-2004 SVT Lightning. Package requisites include a regular cab, 6.5″-bed configuration, and this example is finished in Race Red (PQ) with SVT Lightning Tribute badging. Further exterior equipment includes a folding tonneau cover, a carpeted bedliner, a receiver hitch, and rear parking sensors.

A decal underneath the left headlight denotes the truck as the third SVT Lightning Tribute example produced.

The truck rides on a lowered suspension as part of the SVT Lightning Tribute package, which also added 22″ five-spoke wheels. They are mounted with 305/45 Goodyear tires. Braking is handled by silver-finished calipers fitted over ventilated discs at each corner.

The regular cab houses a 40/20/40 bench trimmed in light gray sueded microfiber with black leather inserts and Lightning backrest logos in homage to the SVT Lightning. The right-side dash has a Lightning Tribute plaque, and the Trailer Tow Package added an integrated trailer brake controller. Other interior appointments include SYNC 4, navigation, cruise control, air conditioning, and WeatherTech floor mats.

The leather-wrapped steering wheel fronts a 120-mph speedometer, a tachometer, a configurable digital display, and auxiliary gauges for oil pressure, coolant temperature, fuel level, and transmission temperature. The driver is aided by a rearview camera, Ford Co-Pilot 360 2.0, and cruise control. The digital odometer indicates 6k miles, approximately 800 of which were added under current ownership.

The 5.0-liter Coyote V8 has been modified by Pioneer Ford of Bremen, Georgia, with their SVT Lightning Tribute package. It includes a Roush Phase 2 supercharger system that has an aftermarket air-intake system, an aluminum intercooler, a 60mm dual-bore throttle body, and Roush high-flow fuel injectors. Output is reported to be 650 horsepower. The engine bay features a signature with “#03 SVT” below it.

Power is routed to the rear wheels through a 10-speed automatic transmission and an electronically locking rear differential with 3.31:1 gears. The truck has a 3″ MBRP cat-back exhaust system with dual side-exit outlets in SVT Lightning Tribute specification.

The digital window sticker lists factory colors, equipment, and a total manufacturer’s suggested retail price of $37,870.

The Carfax report shows no accidents or other damage. It also lists at least one open recall.
